== Description ==

With Snoobi for Wordpress plug in you can install Snoobi Analytics to your site or blog without touching any source codes. Plug in also gives you some fancy reports about visitors in your blog or site.

Please note that this plug in uses Highcharts JavaScript library. In order to use this plug in for commercial website(s), Hicharts licence and pricing policies may apply. Read more at http://www.highcharts.com/license

Key features:

* Install Snoobi Analytics to your blog without touching your source codes
* Figure the most popular pages in your blog / site
* Figure out the keywords that drive the most of the traffic to your blog / site

Read more about the plugin @ [www.snoobi.com](http://www.snoobi.com/media+and+press/press+releases/snoobi+released+updated+wordpress+plugin+with+automated+tracking/)

== Installation ==

1. Upload "snoobi-for-wordpress/" and it's contents to the "/wp-content/plugins/" directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. If you don't have a Snoobi Analytics account, sign up of for a free Snoobi Analytics trial
1. Connect your Wordpress with Snoobi using oAuth
1. Remove existing (if any) installations of Snoobi Analytics from your source codes and plugins
1. Choose which account to use in your Wordpress installation, enable Snoobi-tracking and save the settings

If you opened Snoobi trial, please note following points

1. As soon as you get the login credentials to Snoobi Analytics, connect your Wordpress with Snoobi Analytics
1. Go to the plugin settings page & save once to activate the tracking and save the Snoobi account to use
1. From Snoobi Analytics settings (Settings » Account » Included domains), check that your domain exists in under Included domains list
